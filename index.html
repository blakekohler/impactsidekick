Born from mission, built for impact

Impact Sidekick was founded by the team behind Pulse For Good, a company that has spent over a decade helping nonprofits and social service providers collect anonymous feedback from vulnerable populations.

Through that work, we've seen firsthand how resource-constrained mission-driven organizations are—and how transformative the right technology can be when it's actually accessible.

That's why we built Impact Sidekick: to bring the same thoughtful, human-centered approach to AI modernization.
